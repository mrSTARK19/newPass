Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your data when you use our bus pass application.

2. Information Collection

Personal Information: We collect information such as your name, photo, and other personal details.

3. Use of Information

Collected information is used to generate bus passes, verify identity, and enhance app functionality.

4. Information Sharing

Personal data is only shared with respective authorized officers.

5. Data Security

We take appropriate measures, such as encryption, to protect personal data.

6. User Rights

Users have the right to access, modify, or delete their information within the app.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our privacy practices, please reach out to us at support@studentfare.in.

About Us

We created this app to simplify public transportation for students by providing a quick and convenient way to access and manage bus passes. Our mission is to make commuting easier and more efficient, helping students travel without the hassle of physical tickets. We are committed to creating user-friendly and reliable tools for the community.
